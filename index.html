The idea behind Sorted

A small voice
in the back of
your head.

You know the one. It asks: “Am I actually managing my money right?” We built Sorted because that voice deserves a real answer.

Having access to products
isn’t the same as having your finances sorted.

Here’s what nobody tells you: the average person between 25 and 45 is juggling roughly six financial things at once — a SIP here, an FD there, EPF somewhere, insurance maybe, credit card definitely, tax filing annually and reluctantly.

6+
financial things the average person juggles at once, with no one to help connect them
290M
Indians in exactly this situation — earning, trying, quietly overwhelmed
0
apps that actually tie it all together and just handle it

It’s not about missing returns.
It’s about the weight you carry.

We talk about financial stress in terms of portfolios and interest rates. But the real damage is quieter. It lives in your working memory — that background hum of unresolved things. ITR pending. Insurance unreviewed. PF untouched since your last job.

“A slow-burning anxiety that never quite goes away. One that shows up as doom-scrolling at midnight and a vague, uncomfortable feeling that you’re probably not doing enough.”

The cruel irony is that the people most affected — earning, building, trying to do the right thing — are exactly the people who have the most to gain from getting this right.

Not a dashboard.
A person who just handles it.

Nobody said “I wish I had better charts.” Nobody asked for more notifications or a prettier app. What they described — in different words, every time — was a person. Someone who knows their situation, remembers what they said last month, understands what matters to them, and just handles things without needing to be asked twice.

The best financial advice most people ever received came from a family friend who happened to know finance. Someone who knew them as a person first, and as a client second. That’s what we’re building — for everyone.

The timing
is right.

The tools to do this have finally arrived. For the first time, it’s possible to build something that genuinely understands you — your goals, your history, your risk tolerance, even your procrastinations — and actually does the work instead of just showing you more information.

Every financial product you need already exists. What’s been missing is someone who ties it all together for you, remembers what matters, and handles the things you keep putting off.

Because someone
had to.

We’ve all had that moment of looking at a financial decision and wishing we had someone to call. Someone who’d give it to us straight, remember what we talked about last year, and help us actually do something about it — not just think about it.

The need is real. And India deserves better than what currently exists.

If any of this resonates — if you’ve felt that quiet financial anxiety — we’d love you to be part of what we’re building.
